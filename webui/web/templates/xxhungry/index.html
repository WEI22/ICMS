ICMS

Intelligent Automated Crops Monitoring System

An A.I Driven agriculture tool to identify pests and monitor crops using cameraand image recognition. In this system, users will received real-time notification about the type of pests on the crops and the recommended solution as soon as possible to reduce losses!

ICMS is an Integrated Design Project (IDP) project by a group of 4 undergraduate electrical engineering students who are Khaw Lin Koon, Lim Hong Wei, Tan Xin Xiu, and Chin Kai Li from Universiti Malaya under the supervision of Dr Anis Salwa Binti Mohd Khairuddin.
